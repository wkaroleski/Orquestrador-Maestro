# Research and Synthesis

Define the question and freshness requirement. Prefer primary sources, official repositories, specifications, changelogs, papers, and maintainer documentation. Record publication and access dates, separate facts from inference, deduplicate claims, compare options using explicit criteria, and flag uncertain or conflicting evidence. End with a recommendation, rejected alternatives, and links close to each claim.
