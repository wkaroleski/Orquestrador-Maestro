# Architecture Decision Record

Create concise ADRs with status, date, context, decision, drivers, alternatives considered, consequences, risks, verification, and follow-ups. Link to source files, issues, tests, and operational evidence. Do not turn unresolved discussion into a decision; mark it as proposed until the owner approves.
